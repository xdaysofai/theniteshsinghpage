About Me

Hi, my name is Nitesh Singh, curator of xdaysofWeb3. My first introduction to cryptocurrency occurred on August 16, 2010, with Bitcoin's first hack event.

Since then, I have been maintaining xdaysofWeb3 as a personal diary to keep track of it for my friends and family (AdSense used to hate cryptocurrency). Though I registered the domain in 2025, I was surprised that someone left this awesome domain.

I also set up a Bitcoin full node (still running) on August 15, 2010, to investigate the Value Overflow incident, which (according to folks I’ve met since) may have been India’s first.

For me, Bitcoin was never a “eureka” moment. It was an invitation—to learn tech, explore maths, understand finance and global economics, and dive into probability, topology, macro theory, flying cars, robotics… and to document it along the way.

Apart from that, I also founded Team SOS India Foundation, a volunteer-driven legal aid and disaster-relief network. I served as its first President until 2025.
